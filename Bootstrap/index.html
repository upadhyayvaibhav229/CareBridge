How Care Bridge Foundation Can Help

If you or a loved one are in need of financial assistance for elderly care in Tucson or need assistance submitting an application for ALTCS, the Care Bridge Foundation can help. The foundation’s mission is to ensure that Arizona’s low-income aging and/or disabled individuals receive the care they need and deserve throughout the Medicaid application process by providing advocacy, interim care subsidies, and placement assistance.
